MAQE Homework Challenges

Welcome to the MAQE Engineering candidate testing area.

If you wish to apply as an Engineer at MAQE please follow the instructions below and do the appropriate tests.

When you have finished all the required tests email your completed files (including your resume and cover letter) to recruit+github@maqe.com.

(If you would like more information on MAQE's tech stack and capabilities you can see more details here.)

If you are applying as a Back End Engineer please do the following tests:

  • MAQE Bot
  • Template and Styling

If you are applying as a Front End Engineer please do the following tests:

  • CSS Styling
  • Template and Styling

We do not have dedicated Fullstack Engineer positions at MAQE but if you are interested in showing us your full range of skills you can do the following tests. Also please tell us in your cover letter if you are more Back End or Front End.

  • CSS Styling
  • MAQE Bot
  • Template and Styling

To see all open positions please check our website MAQE.
